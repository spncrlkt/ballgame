# Ballgame — Teams & Art Design Reference

## Concept

16 teams across 8 rivalries. Each team represents a historical culture (pre-1850) with authentic materials, technology, and a unique gameplay mechanic. Teams are paired as thematic opposites.

---

## Rivalries Overview

| Pair | Team A | Team B | Tension |
|------|--------|--------|---------|
| River Kingdoms | Kemet (Egypt) | Akkad (Mesopotamia) | Order vs Conquest |
| Classical Rivals | Hellas (Greece) | Roma (Rome) | Excellence vs Spectacle |
| Silk Road | Tang (China) | Abbasid (Islamic) | Harmony vs Precision |
| Northern Ways | Norðmenn (Viking) | Rus' (Kievan) | Chaos vs Adaptation |
| New World | Azteca (Mesoamerican) | Haudenosaunee (Iroquois) | Dominion vs Unity |
| Gardens & Thrones | Al-Andalus (Moorish) | Crusader (European) | Elegance vs Force |
| Rising Suns | Edo (Japan) | Joseon (Korea) | Flow vs Balance |
| Ocean & Drum | Nso' (West African) | Moana (Polynesian) | Rhythm vs Momentum |

## Team Data

### 1. Kemet — "Scales of Ma'at"
**Era:** 3000–30 BCE | **Region:** Egypt

**Colors:**
- Primary: Lapis blue `(0.15, 0.25, 0.6)`
- Secondary: Gold `(0.85, 0.7, 0.25)`
- Accent: Carnelian `(0.75, 0.3, 0.15)`

**Materials:** Copper, bronze, gold, linen, papyrus, acacia wood, faience

**Implement:** Was-scepter (forked staff, 4-5ft, bronze caps)

**Ball:** Linen-wrapped papyrus core, light, trailing wisps, painted eye

**Stats:** Speed ★★★ | Power ★★★ | Control ★★★★★ | Defense ★★★★

**Mechanic — Judgment:** Finesse shots = double points. Brute force = "sin" markers. Too many sins spawns Ammit hazard.

**Visual Notes:** White linen kilts, gold usekh collar, kohl eyes, jackal/falcon masks, shaved heads

---

### 2. Akkad — "The Lion Throne"
**Era:** 3500–539 BCE | **Region:** Mesopotamia

**Colors:**
- Primary: Royal blue `(0.1, 0.2, 0.5)`
- Secondary: Tawny gold `(0.75, 0.6, 0.3)`
- Accent: Brick red `(0.6, 0.3, 0.2)`

**Materials:** Bronze, iron (late), gold, wool, mudbrick, cedar, bitumen

**Implement:** Mace-staff (bronze lion/star head, cedar shaft, hooked end)

**Ball:** Leather over bitumen core, heavy, cuneiform symbols, roars when struck hard

**Stats:** Speed ★★ | Power ★★★★★ | Control ★★★ | Defense ★★★★

**Mechanic — The Hunt:** One opponent designated "prey" each play. Bonus points for scoring while prey holds ball. Prey rotates.

**Visual Notes:** Tiered wool robes, curled beards, conical helmets, gold jewelry, lion motifs

---

### 3. Hellas — "The Gymnasium"
**Era:** 800–146 BCE | **Region:** Greece

**Colors:**
- Primary: Terracotta red `(0.75, 0.4, 0.3)`
- Secondary: Black `(0.1, 0.1, 0.1)`
- Accent: Olive gold `(0.7, 0.65, 0.35)`

**Materials:** Bronze, iron, marble, olive wood, wool, linen, olive oil

**Implement:** Bare hands + cestus (leather wraps with bronze studs)

**Ball:** Small leather sphere stuffed with hair/feathers, oiled surface, meander patterns

**Stats:** Speed ★★★★ | Power ★★★ | Control ★★★★ | Defense ★★★

**Mechanic — Arete:** Style points for complex/athletic plays. Full meter = one guaranteed perfect action.

**Visual Notes:** Nude/near-nude, oiled skin, athletic builds, leather hand wraps, olive wreaths

---

### 4. Roma — "The Condemned"
**Era:** 753 BCE–476 CE | **Region:** Rome

**Colors:**
- Primary: Imperial crimson `(0.6, 0.15, 0.12)`
- Secondary: Sand/bone `(0.85, 0.8, 0.65)`
- Accent: Bronze `(0.7, 0.55, 0.25)`

**Materials:** Iron, bronze, concrete, marble, wool, leather

**Implement:** Role-based (Murmillo: gladius-bat + scutum / Retiarius: net + trident / Thraex: curved bat + parma)

**Ball:** Small hard leather (pila), sand-roughened, shows blood

**Stats:** Speed ★★★ | Power ★★★★ | Control ★★★ | Defense ★★★★★

**Mechanic — The Crowd:** Exciting plays fill favor meter. Full = buffs. Empty = "Thumbs Down" debuffs.

**Visual Notes:** Gladiator gear varies by role, leather harness, greaves, oiled sand-dusted skin, scars

---

### 5. Tang — "The Celestial Court"
**Era:** 618–907 CE | **Region:** China

**Colors:**
- Primary: Imperial yellow `(0.9, 0.75, 0.3)`
- Secondary: Jade green `(0.4, 0.7, 0.5)`
- Accent: Cinnabar red `(0.7, 0.25, 0.2)`

**Materials:** Steel, bronze, silk, porcelain, bamboo, lacquer, paper

**Implement:** Feet only (silk-wrapped leather boots with concealed steel toe)

**Ball:** Leather panels over air-filled bladder, very light, dragon/phoenix motifs

**Stats:** Speed ★★★★ | Power ★★ | Control ★★★★★ | Defense ★★★

**Mechanic — Harmony:** Passing builds "qi" meter. Full qi = flow state (slow-motion timing). Broken passes drain rapidly.

**Visual Notes:** Silk hanfu fitted for movement, wrapped legs, jade pendants, topknots, both genders play

---

### 6. Abbasid — "The House of Wisdom"
**Era:** 750–1258 CE | **Region:** Islamic Golden Age

**Colors:**
- Primary: Caliphate black `(0.08, 0.08, 0.1)`
- Secondary: Gold `(0.85, 0.7, 0.3)`
- Accent: Turquoise `(0.25, 0.65, 0.7)`

**Materials:** Damascus steel, brass, gold, cotton, silk, glass, cedar

**Implement:** Chogan (polo mallet adapted for ground, brass-inlaid, astrolabe-inspired head)

**Ball:** Leather with brass geometric inlays, 12-pointed star, chimes when struck

**Stats:** Speed ★★★ | Power ★★★★ | Control ★★★★★ | Defense ★★★

**Mechanic — Algebra:** Invisible mathematical grid on court. Golden ratio paths = speed bonus. Calculated angles = accuracy bonus.

**Visual Notes:** Flowing robes over trousers, turbans, geometric embroidery, curved daggers (decorative)

---

### 7. Norðmenn — "The Einherjar"
**Era:** 793–1066 CE | **Region:** Scandinavia

**Colors:**
- Primary: Ice blue `(0.55, 0.7, 0.8)`
- Secondary: Dried blood `(0.45, 0.12, 0.1)`
- Accent: Tarnished silver `(0.6, 0.6, 0.55)`

**Materials:** Bog iron, steel (limited), silver, wool, fur, leather, oak, ash, bone/antler

**Implement:** Knattgildra (ash wood bat, iron-banded) + bare fist

**Ball:** Stone core leather-wrapped, heaviest in game, cracks ice, leaves dents

**Stats:** Speed ★★ | Power ★★★★★ | Control ★★ | Defense ★★★★

**Mechanic — Ragnarök:** Violence (checks, blocks, contested balls) builds meter. Full = Berserker state (faster, stronger, cannot pass).

**Visual Notes:** Wool tunics, fur cloaks, chainmail, braided hair/beards, rune tattoos, Thor's hammer pendants

---

### 8. Rus' — "The Varangian Guard"
**Era:** 882–1240 CE | **Region:** Kievan Rus'

**Colors:**
- Primary: Forest green `(0.2, 0.35, 0.2)`
- Secondary: Byzantine purple `(0.35, 0.15, 0.3)`
- Accent: Gold `(0.8, 0.65, 0.3)`

**Materials:** Iron, steel, gold, wool, linen, fur, birch, Byzantine silk (imported)

**Implement:** Lapta bat (flat birchwood, 2-3ft, carved with runes and crosses)

**Ball:** Leather stuffed with horsehair, medium weight, leaves trail in snow

**Stats:** Speed ★★★ | Power ★★★★ | Control ★★★★ | Defense ★★★★

**Mechanic — Two Faiths:** Switch between "Norse mode" (aggressive) and "Byzantine mode" (defensive) mid-match. Switching costs time but enables tactics.

**Visual Notes:** Norse + Byzantine hybrid, long tunics, fur-lined cloaks, mix of Thor's hammers and Orthodox crosses

---

### 9. Azteca — "Children of the Fifth Sun"
**Era:** 1325–1521 CE | **Region:** Mesoamerica

**Colors:**
- Primary: Turquoise `(0.2, 0.7, 0.75)`
- Secondary: Obsidian black `(0.08, 0.08, 0.1)`
- Accent: Gold `(0.85, 0.7, 0.3)`

**Materials:** Gold, silver, copper (no iron), obsidian, jade, cotton, rubber, feathers

**Implement:** Stone yoke (yugo, 15-30lbs, worn at hip) + manopla (hand stone)

**Ball:** Solid rubber, heavy, dangerous, unpredictable bounce, sun glyph branded

**Stats:** Speed ★★★ | Power ★★★★★ | Control ★★ | Defense ★★★

**Mechanic — Solar Charge:** Ball glows brighter during long rallies. Azteca gain power bonus at high charge.

**Visual Notes:** Bare chest, loincloth, stone yoke, leather guards, jaguar/eagle insignia, feathered headdress (captain)

---

### 10. Haudenosaunee — "The Creator's Game"
**Era:** 1142 CE–present | **Region:** Northeast North America

**Colors:**
- Primary: Red ochre `(0.6, 0.22, 0.12)`
- Secondary: Forest black `(0.1, 0.1, 0.08)`
- Accent: Copper `(0.7, 0.45, 0.25)`

**Materials:** Native copper (cold-worked), deer hide, hickory, birch bark, bone, quills

**Implement:** Lacrosse crosse (bent hickory, 3-4ft, leather/sinew net pocket, eagle feathers)

**Ball:** Deerhide stuffed with hair, small/light/fast, clan symbols, smoke trails when blessed

**Stats:** Speed ★★★★★ | Power ★★ | Control ★★★★ | Defense ★★

**Mechanic — The Pack:** Proximity to teammates = bonuses. Passing chains build "Spirit" meter. Max Spirit = team-wide speed boost.

**Visual Notes:** Minimal clothing, extensive war paint, feathers in hair, shell/bone jewelry, wolf-pack movement

---

### 11. Al-Andalus — "The Garden's Edge"
**Era:** 711–1492 CE | **Region:** Moorish Spain

**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep teal `(0.1, 0.4, 0.45)`
- Secondary: Pomegranate red `(0.55, 0.15, 0.2)`
- Accent: Brass `(0.7, 0.55, 0.35)`

**Materials:** Toledo steel, brass, silver, silk, cotton, cedar, azulejo tiles, lusterware

**Implement:** Cesta (curved wicker basket-glove) + curved blade-racket (secondary)

**Ball:** Leather with brass geometric inlays, 12-pointed star, nearly silent in flight

**Stats:** Speed ★★★★★ | Power ★★★ | Control ★★★★ | Defense ★★

**Mechanic — Geometry:** Invisible optimal paths on court. Following them = bonuses. Rewards spatial awareness.

**Visual Notes:** Flowing sirwal trousers, fitted tunics, geometric embroidery, turbans, silver filigree, pointed shoes

---

### 12. Crusader — "The Iron Cross"
**Era:** 1099–1291 CE | **Region:** Crusader States

**Colors:**
- Primary: Blood red `(0.6, 0.12, 0.1)`
- Secondary: White/silver `(0.9, 0.88, 0.85)`
- Accent: Gold `(0.8, 0.65, 0.25)`

**Materials:** Steel, iron, gold, wool, linen, chainmail, oak, ash

**Implement:** Tournament mace (flanged iron head, cross pommel) + heater shield

**Ball:** Leather over wood, heavy, cross branded

**Stats:** Speed ★★ | Power ★★★★★ | Control ★★ | Defense ★★★★★

**Mechanic — Deus Vult:** Aggression builds fervor meter. Full = "Crusade" mode (immune to knockback, bonus damage, cannot retreat).

**Visual Notes:** Surcoats over mail, great helms, heraldic shields, crosses everywhere, weathered/battle-worn

---

### 13. Edo — "The Floating World"
**Era:** 1603–1868 CE | **Region:** Japan

**Colors:**
- Primary: Indigo blue `(0.15, 0.2, 0.4)`
- Secondary: Cherry blossom pink `(0.85, 0.65, 0.7)`
- Accent: Gold leaf `(0.85, 0.75, 0.35)`

**Materials:** Tamahagane steel, silk, cotton, porcelain, hinoki cypress, bamboo, lacquer

**Implement:** Feet only + tessen (iron war fan for deflection)

**Ball:** Deer leather panels over air, very light/floaty, cherry blossom embroidered

**Stats:** Speed ★★★★ | Power ★★ | Control ★★★★★ | Defense ★★★★

**Mechanic — Wa (Harmony):** Keeping ball airborne builds meter. Ground contact resets. Full Wa = time slows, perfect strikes available.

**Visual Notes:** Hakama + gi, tabi socks, topknot, clan mon, refined minimal aesthetic

---

### 14. Joseon — "The Scholar-Warriors"
**Era:** 1392–1897 CE | **Region:** Korea

**Colors:**
- Primary: Celadon green `(0.45, 0.6, 0.5)`
- Secondary: Scholar white `(0.92, 0.9, 0.85)`
- Accent: Royal red `(0.65, 0.2, 0.2)`

**Materials:** Steel, iron, ramie, silk, pine, white porcelain, mother-of-pearl

**Implement:** Jukjanggu (curved stick, 3-4ft, mother-of-pearl inlay)

**Ball:** Leather with air pocket, taeguk symbol, predictable bounce

**Stats:** Speed ★★★ | Power ★★★★ | Control ★★★★ | Defense ★★★★

**Mechanic — Yin-Yang:** Aggressive actions charge defensive meter; defensive actions charge offensive. Must cycle to access powered abilities.

**Visual Notes:** Simplified hanbok, dopo elements, gat hat (captain), wrapped boots, jade/silver ornaments

---

### 15. Nso' — "The Bronze Echo"
**Era:** 1200–1850 CE | **Region:** West Africa

**Colors:**
- Primary: Kente gold `(0.85, 0.7, 0.2)`
- Secondary: Indigo `(0.15, 0.12, 0.35)`
- Accent: Bronze `(0.65, 0.5, 0.3)`

**Materials:** Bronze (advanced lost-wax), iron, gold, cotton, kente cloth, ebony, terracotta

**Implement:** Wooden striker (2-3ft, bronze inlay, drum-resonant) + iron wrist rings

**Ball:** Leather over hollow wood, resonates like talking drum, different pitches at different spots, cowrie inlays

**Stats:** Speed ★★★★ | Power ★★★★ | Control ★★★ | Defense ★★★

**Mechanic — The Rhythm:** Constant drum beat underlies play. On-beat actions = empowered. Off-beat = weakened. Master the rhythm.

**Visual Notes:** Kente cloth wraps, bare torsos, bronze arm bands, ankle bells, ritual scarification, wooden masks (ceremony)

---

### 16. Moana — "The Wave Riders"
**Era:** 1000–1850 CE | **Region:** Polynesia

**Colors:**
- Primary: Ocean teal `(0.15, 0.55, 0.6)`
- Secondary: Volcanic black `(0.12, 0.12, 0.1)`
- Accent: Coral red-orange `(0.8, 0.35, 0.25)`

**Materials:** None metal (stone-age), basalt, obsidian, greenstone/pounamu, tapa cloth, koa wood, bone

**Implement:** Patu (short flat club, greenstone/wood/bone) + woven net (secondary)

**Ball:** Woven fiber over stone core, heavy, unpredictable weight, splashes on impact, ocean patterns

**Stats:** Speed ★★★ | Power ★★★★★ | Control ★★★ | Defense ★★★★

**Mechanic — The Tide:** Consecutive successes build momentum. Peak tide = massively empowered action. Any failure crashes to zero.

**Visual Notes:** Tapa cloth wraps, extensive tā moko (facial tattoos), bone/shell jewelry, shark teeth, powerful builds, haka stance
